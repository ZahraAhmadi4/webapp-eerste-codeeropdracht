The big Carnaval party

Carnaval is being hugely celebrated in the party room Q-Factory. You can come dressed as whatever you want. There are lots of drinks and food. Are you in the mood to celebrate this holiday and party your way all through the night, then this may be a wonderful event for you.

From 23:00 - 04:00

Address: Q-Factory Amsterdam, Altantisplein 1,
1093NE Amsterdam
