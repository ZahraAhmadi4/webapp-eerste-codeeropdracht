Activities

Op April the 27th the Dutch celebrate the birthday of the King. All over the country there are plenty of activities to do and participate in. Most people will go out to see the King and the Queen.

Urban Music Festival Amsterdam

During the Urban Music Festival in Amsterdam lots of Dutch artists come to the stage to perform. There are lots of tents where you can buy yourself a drink and food. For the entrance you have to buy a ticket.

€ 39,50 - 50Buy your ticket here.
Date: 27 April

Address: Turbinestraat 12, 1014 AV Amsterdam

From Central Station you can take bus 22 to Sloterdijk. You have to get off at the stop Contactweg. And with four minutes walking, you'll be there.
