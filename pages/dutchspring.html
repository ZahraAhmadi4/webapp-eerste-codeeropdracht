King's Day (Koningsdag)

Koningsdag is celebrated on King Willem Alexander's birthday, 27 April. The King and members of the Royal Family pay a public visit to one or two cities. Each year the celebrations will be held in another city.

Liberation Day (Bevrijdingsdag)

On May the 5th, the Dutch celebrate the end of the Second World War and the Liberations of the Netherlands in 1945.
