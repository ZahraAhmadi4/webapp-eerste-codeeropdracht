Diwali Festival

The Diwali Festival is a big party in the Hindi era. Of course you don't have to be Hindi yourself, you can come to celebrate this festival alongside your friends. At the festival you'll see a diversity of dance and music styles. The people who are dancing are dressed in their traditional clothing.

€ 0,
Date: 12 October
from 12:00 - 19:00

Address: Stadsplein Amstelveen

From Central station, you can travel with metro 54 to the stop Amsterdam, Bijlmer Arena. And then you have to get bus 171 to the stop Binnenhof, Amstelveen. From there, it will be a one minute walk.
