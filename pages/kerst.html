The Amsterdam winter Paradise

The Amsterdam winter Paradise is a winter world paradise where people can skate and play their inner child out on ice. You have the following activities in the Amsterdam winter paradise:

  • Skate
  • Tubeslides
  • Ice Racing
  • Dance Floor
  • Winter Games
  • Cross Country Skiing

€ 7 - 39,50,
Opening times: 21 December 2019 - 5 January 2020

Address: Europaplein 22, 1078 GZ Amsterdam

From Central station you can travel with metro 52 to station Zuid. You have to get off at the stop Europaplein. From there it will be a three minute walk.
